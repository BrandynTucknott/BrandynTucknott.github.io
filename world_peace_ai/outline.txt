WORLD PEACE AI OUTLINE

Section I: Linear Stages
    These stages are designed to have a well-defined start and end. The order in which these stages occur does not matter.
    Vision: stages are straightforward with minimal evolution

Section II: Non-Linear Stages
    These stages are designed to have a many different starting paths, but an unclear end goal
    Vision: stages have multiple viable starting paths which all lead to the same end (unkown to the player)

Section III: End Game Stages
    These stages have a clear end goal
    Vision: The player will win the game here. Timed Stage (not determined as to (timed for score) or (time limit))
